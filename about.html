The Barber

Joel Mathew

What's up! I'm Joel, the founder and sole barber at JayyBl3nz. Barbering isn't just my job, it's my passion and my art form. Every client who sits in my chair gets my full attention and expertise.

I started cutting hair as a sophomore in highscool, learning from YouTube tutorials and practicing on friends and family. What began as a way to save money on haircuts quickly became an obsession. I fell in love with the craft - the precision, the creativity, and most importantly, the confidence a great haircut gives someone.

After years of perfecting my skills and building a loyal clientele, I launched JayyBl3nz to offer a more personal, one-on-one barbering experience. No rushing, no distractions - just quality cuts in a relaxed environment. I also started curating vintage and contemporary clothing because style doesn't stop at the hairline.

What I Bring to the Chair

Precision

Every line, every fade, every detail matters. I take my time to ensure your cut is clean and exactly what you envisioned.

Communication

I listen first, cut second. Bring references, describe what you want, or let me suggest something that fits your style.

Consistency

Come back every two weeks or every two months - you'll get the same quality and attention to detail every single time.

The JayyBl3nz Experience

When you book with me, you're not just getting a haircut. You're getting a personalized experience in a comfortable, private setting where you can relax and leave looking your absolute best.

I work by appointment only to ensure every client gets my undivided attention. No waiting in line, no rushed cuts, no distractions. Just great conversation and an even better haircut.
